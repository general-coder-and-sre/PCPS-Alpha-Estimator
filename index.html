Retirement Tips

🗓️ Timing Your Retirement

Early retirement factors are calculated using your age in complete years and months. Part months are discarded. If you were born on the 17th, your factor improves on the 17th of each month. Make your monthly birthday annivesary your last day of work to maximise your pension.

💰 Classic Scheme & Indexation

Classic has an automatic tax-free lump sum. While "inverse commutation" (exchanging lump sum for more pension) exists, this calculator doesn't support it. Note that your pension is index-linked (based on September CPI) and increases for life.

⚖️ McCloud Remedy & Your ABS

If you are affected by the McCloud Remedy, you should have two Annual Benefit Statements (ABS) representing your different options. To compare them, use this tool to calculate your first option, note the results, and then repeat with the figures from your second statement.

🏦 Tax & Take-Home Pay

Your pension is subject to Income Tax, but you no longer pay National Insurance (NI) or pension contributions. You may find you need less gross income in retirement than you did while working.
